## Requirements

Wireit is supported on Linux, macOS, and Windows.

Wireit is supported on Node Current (18), Active LTS (16), and the most recent
Maintenance LTS (14). See [Node releases](https://nodejs.org/en/about/releases/)
for the schedule.
